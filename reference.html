Engineering

Learning From Every Millimetre Of Road

V12 Vantage has a dynamically enriched transmission. The eight-speed ZF automatic gearbox has been tuned specifically to the complex character of the car. Employing bespoke software to ensure fast and dynamic gear shifting, coupled with refinement and usability past the possibilities of a conventional double clutch. With every millimetre between its tyres, it learns more about the road conditions. Meaning it’s always in the right gear at the right time. Ready, set, poised to thrill.

It was perhaps inevitable that a fully race-honed version should result. In 2012 the V12 Vantage GT3 made its debut with a career would last seven seasons and net four British GT Championships in the UK alone.
